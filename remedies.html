    Acne

    Aloe Vera

    Apply the jelly from the plant regularly to help cure acne infection. The astringent properties of the plant that are activated when applied to the skin help cure acne and blemishes with little or no visible scarring.

    Mix 1tbs honey with 1/8 teaspoon turmeric powder. Apply to affected areas and leave for 20-35 minutes. Rinse with warm water, apply moisturiser or coconut oil. Repeat regularly.

    Cramp

    Magnesium, Potassium

    There are a number of ways to help ease muscles when they are cramped. It is important to stretch the muscle as much as possible as the cramp is occurring. To help prevent the cramps either taking supplements or increasing magnesium and potassium in your diet will help. Good sources of magnesium can be found in whole-grain breads and cereals, as well as nuts and beans. Potassium is found in most fruits and vegetables, especially bananas, oranges and rockmelon.

    Dandruff

    Apple Cider Vinegar, Water

    Add equal quantities of ACV with water to make 1/2 - 1 cup of solution. Pour the mixture onto the scalp (best in shower) and massage for a few minutes. Rinse well. A few drops of tea tree oil can be added to the mixture if the smell of the vinegar is off-putting.
